ANNE SEIDLITZ

Documentary film and development writer

[full website in the works!]

I write scripts, grant proposals, narration, and other materials for documentary films, many
of which have been broadcast on PBS and other networks, and screened at major film festivals. NEH and NEA proposals, and those from other sources, have secured major funding for films. My specialty area is on American culture, history, and the arts, but I have also written extensively for films on contemporary social issues.

Development writing has been for major arts, cultural, and community development organizations and institutions in the New York area and beyond.

I live in Hudson, New York.
